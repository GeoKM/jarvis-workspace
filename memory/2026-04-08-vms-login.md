What *is* plausible is this:

## The real suspect
Your system may be using **external authentication** or a related mechanism, in which case:

- the password stored in `SYSUAF` is **not necessarily the password used to verify login**
- OpenVMS may still use `SYSUAF` for quotas/UIC/profile, but authenticate elsewhere
- a local password copy in `SYSUAF` can exist yet still not be the authoritative login password

That behaviour is documented for OpenVMS 7.3 era systems. Source: <https://www.zx.net.nz/mirror/h71000.www7.hp.com/doc/73final/documentation/pdf/OVMS_73_REL_NOTES.pdf> and <https://www.zx.net.nz/mirror/h30266.www3.hp.com/odl/vax/opsys/vmsos73/vmsos73/6346/6346pro_016.html>
